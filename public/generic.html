GAME RULES

Rules and regulations for playing ASTACHEMMA

The game will be played by using 8 shells as dice, by rolling them we will get different values depending on the face of the shells. each player will be having 8 coins ,the final aim of each player is to make all the coins reach the winning point ,who finishes it first will be the winner ,the board has 5 levels ,we can enter into the second level from first level only after killing the enemies coins,in the 5th level we have winning points ,the coins will be moved in anti-clockwise direction.
